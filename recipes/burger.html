Smash Burger

Description

"Savor our famous smash burger, featuring crispy, griddled beef topped with melted cheese, pickles, and onions on a toasted bun. Perfect for a quick, flavorful meal, it's a classic crowd-pleaser."

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • Hamburger buns
  • American cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Pickles

Instruction:

  1. Heat skillet until smoking hot
  2. Smash 1/2 pound patties on skillet
  3. Add thinly sliced onions on top then flip
  4. Salt to taste
  5. Top with cheese and remove from skillet
  6. Add burger sauce and pickles on bun and plate
