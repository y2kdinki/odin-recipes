Lasagna

Description

Enjoy our classic lasagna, layered with tender pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy b√©chamel. Topped with melted mozzarella and parmesan, it's baked to golden perfection, ideal for a comforting family meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1/2 cup onion
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 1 pack of lasagna noodles
  • 1 tablesoon of salt
  • 3/4 pounds of mozzarella
  • 1 cup of parmesan cheese

Instruction:

  1. Dice 1 onion and sautee until golden
  2. Add ground beef to pot and stir til browned
  3. Remove ground beef mixture and set aside
  4. In a 12x12 baking dish add your first layer of noodles
  5. Add in a layer of beef, sauce, and cheese
  6. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes
  7. Top with parmesan cheese and serve
